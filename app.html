How to set up Telegram
1
Open Telegram → search @BotFather → send /newbot
2
Choose a name e.g. Gold Trader Alerts and username e.g. GoldTraderPro_bot
3
BotFather gives you a token like:
1234567890:ABCdefGHIjklMNO...
Paste this into Bot Token above.
4
Open your new bot → send it any message (e.g. hello)
5
Get your Chat ID: open this URL in browser:
https://api.telegram.org/botTOKEN/getUpdates
Find "id" inside the JSON response. That's your Chat ID.
6
Add your Vercel proxy URL to Vercel env vars:
TELEGRAM_BOT_TOKEN = your-bot-token
TELEGRAM_CHAT_ID = your-chat-id
Then click Save & Activate and Send test.
